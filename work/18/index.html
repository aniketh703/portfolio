World Cancer Day Campaign

DisciplineCampaign Design
Year2024
Stack
CampaignBrandingBehance
LiveView on Behance

A cross-format awareness campaign for World Cancer Day spanning paper cup sleeves and social content touchpoints.

Challenge

Campaign assets needed to communicate empathy and urgency in limited physical and digital space while staying visually consistent across mediums.

Role & Deliverables

Role

Campaign Visual Designer

Deliverables

Cup sleeve artwork, social creatives, and adaptation layouts.

Goal

High awareness with clear emotional resonance.

Execution

01. Message Framing

Simplified copy to short, memorable statements for quick recall.

02. Visual Cohesion

Used a unified color and type language across print and social formats.

03. Adaptation

Designed modular compositions to support multiple platform sizes.

“Awareness design works when people remember the message after the medium is gone.”

Campaign assets across physical and digital channels.

Outcome

The campaign system maintained consistency across touchpoints while preserving the emotional tone required for a public-health awareness initiative.
