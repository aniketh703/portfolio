The real problem

People reach for spiritual apps in fragmented moments, not ideal reading sessions. The job was to make guidance feel available in low-attention, low-bandwidth contexts.

What did not work

That direction increased cognitive load exactly when users needed comfort and speed.

If I did it again

I would prototype usage situations first — commute, fatigue, low signal — and let those constraints drive architecture decisions from day one.
